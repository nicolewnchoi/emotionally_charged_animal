Description

Emotionally Charged Animal (E.C.A.) is your ultimate companion for when you need to express yourself—no matter where you are. Whether you’re feeling overwhelmed, stressed, or just in need of a release, E.C.A. is there to help. Crafted from high-quality recycled materials and featuring the softest fabrics for snuggling, this stuffed animal is both a comforting cuddle buddy and a portable charger powered by your own scream. When you feel the urge to scream, simply scream into E.C.A.’s ear. Its special open-cell polyurethane foam material will muffle the noise, making it perfect for any environment—whether at school, work, or anywhere you need to stay discreet. Your scream will then be converted into energy to power your mobile device, keeping you charged and connected when you need it most.
