ABOUT US

Emotionally Charged Animal (E.C.A.) was born from the vision of a former engineer who found herself navigating life without close connections to share her feelings with. When stress became overwhelming, she discovered comfort in screaming into her stuffed animals—a simple act that provided emotional relief.

Inspired by this experience, E.C.A. combines the power of emotional release with cutting-edge renewable energy technology. By harnessing "scream power," an innovative and sustainable energy source, and integrating it with the soothing embrace of a stuffed animal, E.C.A. was created as a one-of-a-kind charger and companion.

E.C.A. is here to ensure you're always charged—emotionally and literally—offering comfort, connection, and energy when you need it most.

WHAT WE LOVE ABOUT E.C.A.

Softest Materials

Highest quality recyclable soft materials simulate the touch of a real-life animal.

Muffled Noise

Our open-cell polyurethane foam material silences noises up to 130 dB.

3A High-Speed Charging

E.C.A. charges up to 75% of your mobile device up to an hour.

Sustainability

E.C.A.'s source of energy comes from screens and all materials are sustainable for use.
